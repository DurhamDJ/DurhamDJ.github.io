Welcome to DDJS

Durham DJ Society invites you to view all of our members who are actively looking for work in the Durham area. Please take the time to listen to our society's talent, with some of our members' mixes and tracks linked below their profile. If you are looking for a DJ and like what you see on this page, please contact us at dds8djs@durham.ac.uk.

Alternatively, if you're a DJ and would like to get involved, feel free to come to one of our free weekly open decks sessions, every Wednesday at Fabio's bar from 7pm till 10pm. Our members enjoy rental of the society's very own Pioneer XDJ-RR, as well as the option of being promoted on this website and free entry to and DJ time at DDJS events.
